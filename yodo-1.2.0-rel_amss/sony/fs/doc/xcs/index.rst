XCS - Xperia Custom Software
============================

Xperia Custom Software, hereby called XCS, is a service that allows a trusted
Sony Mobile partner to program compatible Xperia devices with their own custom software.
Partners are able to unlock the device bootloader or insert their own root of
trust key in order to get a trusted boot chain.
